Definition of Airport Hub Classification:

Large Hub:
Airports that account for at least 1% of total passenger boarding annually.

Medium Hub:
Airports that account for 0.25% to 1% of total passenger boarding annually.

Small Hub:
Airports that account for 0.05% to 0.25% of total passenger boarding annually.

Non-Hub:
Airports that account for less than 0.05% of total passenger boarding annually.
